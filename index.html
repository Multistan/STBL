    Hi!




    Sir Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web in 1989. Sir Tim is the Director of the3 World Wide Web Consortium (W3C ), a Web standards organization founded in 1994 which develops interoperable technologies (specifications, guidelines, software, and tools) to lead the web to its full potential. He is a Director of the World Wide Web Foundation which ws launched in 2009 to coordinate efforts to further potential of the Web to benefit humanity.

    In 2001 he became a fellow of the Royal Sociey. He has been the recipient of several international awards including the Jpan Price, The Prince of Asturias Foundation Prize, the Mellenuim Technology Prize, and Germany's Die Quadriga award, In 2004 he was knighted by H.M. Queen Elizabeth and in 2007 he was awared the Order of Merit. In 2009 he was elected a foreign associate of the National Academy of Sciences.
